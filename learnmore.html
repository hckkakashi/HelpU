Welcome to HelpU

At HelpU, we believe that mental health matters just as much as physical health.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make mental health care accessible, engaging, and supportive for everyone, especially youth and students who may be struggling with anxiety, overthinking, sadness, loneliness, or addictive behaviors. We aim to break the stigma around mental health by creating a space where users feel heard, valued, and supported.

What You Can Do Here

  • Chatbot for Support: A friendly AI chatbot is available 24/7 to help you calm your mind, check in on your feelings, and give instant suggestions for relief strategies.
  • Mental Health Quiz & Games: Understand yourself better through quick, interactive quizzes and games designed to reflect on your emotional and mental well-being.
  • Talk with a Specialist: Connect with licensed mental health professionals when you're ready to talk. We’re here to listen without judgment.
  • Find Nearby Help: Use our map feature to locate nearby hospitals, counseling centers, or safe spaces that provide mental health support.
  • Relaxation Zone: Watch relaxing videos, listen to calming beats, and follow guided breathing techniques whenever you feel anxious or low.

Privacy & Confidentiality

We deeply value your trust. All your chats and quiz responses remain private and secure. You control what you share, and we never share your data with anyone.

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Struggles with mental health are normal, and help is always available. You're not alone.

Whether you need support, want to understand yourself better, or just need a safe space — we’re here for you.
